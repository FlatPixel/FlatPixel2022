About us

Flat Pixel is a Paris based Game studio made up of artists and engineers dedicated to unique visual experiences and cutting edge technology. Since 2012 we’ve created over 60 games and mini-games in Augmented Reality, acquiring valuable expertise in harnessing its power to create truly fun, novel and polished AR experiences.


We’re a small team that packs a big punch! Along with our squad of international collaborators, we strive to deliver juicy, robust video games to be enjoyed without moderation. We're always thirsty for new encounters; so if this sounds like a match, give us a shout!!
